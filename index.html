What is Quantum?

Classical computers use **bits**—like a light switch that is either ON (1) or OFF (0).

Quantum computers use **qubits**. Because of quantum physics, these qubits can represent a complex mix of 1 and 0 at the same time. This doesn't just make them faster; it makes them capable of shortcutting math problems that are currently "unsolvable."
