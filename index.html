The Glossary

Master the vocabulary of the subatomic world.

Superposition

The ability of a quantum system to be in multiple states at once. Think of a spinning coin that is both heads and tails until it stops.

Entanglement

A "spooky" connection where two particles stay linked regardless of distance. Changing one instantly affects the other.

Qubit

The basic unit of quantum information. Unlike a regular bit (0 or 1), a qubit uses quantum mechanics to hold more data.

Decoherence

When a quantum system loses its "quantumness" due to interference from the environment, like heat or vibrations.

Quantum Gate

The building blocks of quantum circuits. They rotate the state of a qubit to perform calculations.

Bloch Sphere

A geometric representation used to visualize the state of a single qubit as a point on the surface of a sphere.
