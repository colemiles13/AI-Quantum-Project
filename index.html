The Glossary

Superposition

The ability of a quantum system to be in multiple states at once until measured.

Entanglement

A "spooky" connection where particles stay linked regardless of distance.

Decoherence

When quantum information leaks into the environment, causing errors.

Bra-Ket Notation

Standard notation in quantum mechanics (e.g., |0⟩) used to describe quantum states.

QKD

Quantum Key Distribution. Using entanglement to share encryption keys that are physically impossible to intercept.

Noisy Intermediate-Scale Quantum (NISQ)

The current era of quantum computing where chips have 50-1000 qubits but are prone to errors.
